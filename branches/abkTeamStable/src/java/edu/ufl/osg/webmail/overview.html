GatorMail intends to be a very scaleable and very IMAP server friendly webmail.

About

GatorMail is the creation of William A. McArthur, Jr. He started a hobby project to prototype a webmail that would meet the needs of the University of Florida after being disappointed by the then current webmail offerings on the market.
